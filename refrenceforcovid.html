WHY AROGYA SETU
Keep You Safe Keeps your near and dear ones safe Keeps India at large safe
If you make a first or a second degree contact with a COVID-19 positive person, Aarogya Setu alerts you and gets you timely medical help. The self-assessment test also helps you in identifying possibilities of infection. By early identification and prevention of potential risk of infection in you and others, Aarogya Setu enables better safety for those around you, acting as a shield of protection for all your loved ones. The app helps the Govt. of India identify hotspots, hence aiding in curbing the spread of the infection. The more number of people use it, the more effective the app will be in chaining the pandemic.
